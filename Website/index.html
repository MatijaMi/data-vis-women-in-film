Women Film Pioneers Explorer

The Women Film Pioneers Explorer allows you to explore the data collection of the Women Film Pioneers Project (WFPP) through interactive visualizations. Each one offers a particular view on women and film history. The WFPE wants to encourage users to contribute to the WFPP and also reflect on what data means for current film historiography.
Pioneer of the Day

TIMELINE
