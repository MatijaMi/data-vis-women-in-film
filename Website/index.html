Women Film Pioneers Explorer

The Women Film Pioneers Project is a scholarly resource for researching the worldwide participation of women at all levels of film production during the silent era.

On this website, you can explore the data provided to the project through interactive representations. Each of the different representations offers its own perspective and can be used not only to provide an interesting look at the data, but also to show which parts of the data are incomplete. With our contribution to data representation, we hope to encourage people to support the project.

CLUSTER
Women film pioneers also fulfilled a great number of positions in the film industry. To get an overview of these professions, they are demonstrated as clusters, which can be explored thoroughly with their subcategories. An overview of all the pioneers and countries where they worked is also available.
