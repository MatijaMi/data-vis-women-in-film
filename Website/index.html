Women Film Pioneers Explorer

The Women Film Pioneers Explorer allows you to explore the worldwide participation of women in early film production through interactive data visualizations. Each data visualization offers its own view on film history.

The website is based on the collaborative database of the Women Film Pioneers Project (WFPP) created by Jane Gaines, Radha Vatsal, and Monica Dall’Asta. The different visualizations aim to provide new perspectives on the WFPP’s rich collection of research data. Furthermore, they emphasize blind spots and absences of women in film history by pointing out which data is missing.

The Women Film Pioneers Explorer seeks to encourage users to further contribute to the WFPP and also think about what data means for present film historiography.
